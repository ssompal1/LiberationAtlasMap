Welcome to Liberation Atlas!

We welcome you to explore and grapple with the policing of migrant Asian massage work throughout New York City through seeing the various forms of widespread criminalization of massage work and listening to how it affects workers in the city. Liberation Atlas offers an alternative understanding of carceral anti-trafficking organizations own maps which celebrate growing points across a map as evidence of the success of policies which in reality criminalize racialized and gendered low wage work. We invite you to engage these maps and share them as a way of amplifying the struggle and power of migrant massage worker organizing
